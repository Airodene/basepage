Instructions for install:

Once you have downloaded, extract and place all the files included inside your web root.
Your webroot should then look something like this:
- css
- fonts
- img
- scr
- favicon.ico
- index.html
- info.html
- installation.txt
- README.md


Follow our documentation and in-file comments for editing and customisation instructions!
BasePage can also be installed as a page of a larger website, as long as the link points to index.html
